BS Accountancy




The Bachelor of Science in Accountancy (BSA) is a five (5)- year trimestral undergraduate degree program anchored on the school’s three pillars: industry-guided curriculum, industry professionals as faculty, and business-IT tools. The curriculum includes a six-month (two-term), full-time two-term internship program with our industry partners, who are among the top companies in the country to make sure our students get a feel of doing actual industry projects in a real working environment, as well as an integrated and comprehensive review course for all subjects covered in the CPA Licensure Examination.

Utilizing IT-enabled tools, the program aims to produce Certified Public Accountants (CPAs) who are ready for today’s business challenges. The courses offered under the program deal with theoretical and practical applications relating to the different fields of accountancy.

Fields of Accountancy

Financial Accounting
Management Accounting
Auditing
Taxation
Business Law

Career Prospects

BS Accountancy program hones its graduates in preparation for job opportunities in the financial industry. Careers in the financial sector can be in

  • Auditing
  • Management Accounting
  • Financial Analysis
  • Bank Operations
  • Financing
  • Tax Audit
  • Wealth Management

BS Accountancy graduates are equipped with a strong financial and accounting acumen combined with the skill and knowledge of using industry business IT software.
