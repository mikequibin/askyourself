BS Business Administration




The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration degree is designed for students seeking a strong and comprehensive foundation in Business. The program is more structured than other business programs, covering the breadth of business-related disciplines as defined in the business core curriculum - accounting, international business law, economics, finance, management, international business, and information systems.

The program culminates in a cap stone strategy course requiring students to apply their knowledge of the various aspects of business in a market simulation. Graduates are equipped with a strong business acumen combined with the skill and knowledge of using the latest industry business IT software.

BSBA Major in BUSINESS MANAGEMENT

The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration Major in Business Management (BSBA-BM) is a four (4)-year trimestral undergraduate degree program that aims to developed IT enabled business leaders. The program equips the students with management and technical skills necessary for today’s business managers.

The courses offered cover the various functional areas in management, human resources, operations, logistics, marketing and finance.

Career Prospects

Graduates of Business Management can easily get jobs in different functional areas of an organization such as

  • Human Resources
  • Supply Chain Management
  • Logistics
  • Marketing and Sales
  • Operations

BSBA Major in MARKETING & ADVERTISING

The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration Major in Marketing and Advertising (BSBA-MA) is a four (4) year degree program that aims to develop IT-enabled marketing and advertising practitioners. The MA program covers various marketing areas such as product and brand management, marketing services, retail marketing, advertising, public relations and consumer behavior research, as it hones students with technical and practical marketing and advertising skills required by today’s industries.

The courses offered cover the various functional areas of brand and product management, marketing research, marketing communication, sales management, advertising and promotions, public relations, channel distribution management, key accounts management among others

Career Prospects

Graduates of Marketing and Advertising can take career opportunities in different fields of marketing, such as

  • Brand and Product Management
  • Marketing Research
  • Sales Management
  • Advertising and Promotions
  • Channel Distribution Management
  • Key Accounts Management
