BS Computer Engineering




The Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BS CPE) is a four (4) year trimestral undergraduate degree program that aims to produce computer engineers who can design microprocessors and develop embedded systems.

The courses offered cover areas such as software systems on algorithms, data structures and digital logic, switching theory, computer architecture and performance analysis among others.

Field of Specialization covered

Embedded Systems Technology
System & Network Administration
Computer Programming & Interfacing
Industrial Automation & Control
Semiconductor Design
Software Development

Career Prospects

Covering a vast field of specializations from system and network administration to software, robotics and artificial intelligence, Computer Engineering graduates can get jobs as

  • Project Engineer Manager
  • Network Systems Administrator Manager
  • Data Communications Engineer
  • Systems Engineer/Developer
  • Systems Analyst Designer
  • Technical Support Engineer/Manager
  • Quality Assurance Engineer Manager
  • Test Engineer
