BS Computer Science




The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) is a four-year trimestral undergraduate degree program. Its curriculum highlights theories and concepts, algorithmic foundations and implementation and application of information, computing and innovative solutions catering to the needs of the business, society and industry.

The objective of this program is providing students with a unique learning experience through the appropriate combination of theories, hands-on exercises, teamwork and guided project work.

SPECIALIZATIONS:

Software Systems
Cyber Security & Forensics
Network Systems
