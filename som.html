BS Accountancy

Degree program anchored on the school’s three pillars: industry-guided curriculum, industry professionals as faculty, and business-IT tools. The program aims to develop IT-enabled certified public accountants (CPA's) who are well-versed in generally accepted accounting principles (GAAP).

BS Business Administration

Degree program that aims to develop IT-enabled business leaders. The program equips the students with management and technical skills necessary for today’s business managers. It prepares students for a professional career in various functional areas in management, human resources, operations, logistics, marketing and finance.

BS Tourism Management

Degree program that aims to develop managers for the various sector of the tourism industry. The program aims to produce tourism management professionals who are exposed to the latest IT tools used in the tourism industry.

BSBA Risk & Insurance Management

This course will engage the students in discovering various opportunities and applications in the different facets of insurance management that includes that quantitative aspects of risk and insurance operations. The program will further help students prepare in confronting different opportunities and developing awareness of the challenges in today’s financial world.
